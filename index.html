The Good Pie | Our Story

The Good Pie is a restaurant and bar established in 2014, located in the heart of the St. Louis Loop. The Good Pie uses minimal, fresh ingredients and a fire wood oven to craft authentic neopolitan pizza. We are trying to get back to the craft of Naples pizza, with an emphasis on quality, craftsmanship, and simplicity.
